# GOVERNMENT OF BENUE STATE
# BENUE STATE BUREAU OF STATISTICS (BSBS)
## BENUE COMMUNITY MAPPING QUESTIONNAIRE

---

The Benue State Bureau of Statistics (BSBS) is conducting this Community Mapping Exercise to collect accurate and up-to-date information on community resources, infrastructure, services, and development challenges across the State. The information gathered will support evidence-based planning, equitable allocation of resources, and targeted interventions aimed at improving service delivery and living conditions at the community level. This exercise will document key facilities and services such as markets, health and educational institutions, access roads, agricultural and environmental resources, telecommunications and information channels, financial services, security arrangements, and social protection programmes. It will also identify gaps in infrastructure and social services that require government and development partner intervention.

The usefulness of this exercise depends on the accuracy and completeness of the information provided. Responses from community leaders and representatives will directly inform government policies, development programmes, and investments affecting this community. All information provided will be treated with strict confidentiality and used solely for official statistical and planning purposes. Your cooperation and honest participation are highly solicited and appreciated. If you have any questions or require further clarification, please call **0703 055 6316** or **0806 811 4736**.
